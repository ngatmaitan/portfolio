My name is Nicole Gatmaitan. I recently graduated Southern Methodist University with a degree in Computer Science.

Something that fascinates me is how much our lives revolve around technology. However, I know that not everyone is tech-savvy and one of the worst parts about technology is when it breaks or doesn't do what it's supposed to do. I am very interested in UI/UX design and I want to improve the ways that people interact with technology, even by just a little bit. Because of my background, I know all about object-oriented and modular programming using different languages including C++, Java, Python amongst others. I would like to be more proficient in the backend side of development so that I can become a full-stack developer.

I enjoy doing whatever most people in their early twenties do and spend the little free time that I have either watching Netflix or being on tumblr. I like making things look asthetically pleasing. I never really understood the whole "being too focused on what you're doing that you just forget to eat" thing until I started designing. I try to surround myself with people that make me happy because I think one of the best moments in life is when after I hang out with my friends and think back about what we did, I realize how much I appreciate everyone in my life.
